Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026
This is a starting template, not legal advice. Please review with a legal professional before relying on it, especially once you start collecting real payments or customer data.

What we collect

When you use this website to browse the menu or place an order, we do not automatically collect personal data on our servers — this site does not have a backend database. Information you choose to share (name, order details, phone number) is only shared when you send an order via WhatsApp, a phone call, or a UPI payment app, and is handled within those platforms according to their own privacy policies.

Local device storage

To make ordering easier, this website stores a few things directly in your browser (not on our servers): your cart, wishlist, loyalty points, and dark mode preference. This information stays on your device and is not visible to us or anyone else unless you clear your browser data.

Third-party services

Links to WhatsApp, UPI payment apps, Instagram, and Google Maps are provided for your convenience. Once you leave our website through one of these links, that service's own privacy policy applies.

Contact us

If you have questions about this policy, reach out via WhatsApp or email listed on our Contact section.
